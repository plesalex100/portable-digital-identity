### The Problem:
Passengers show their passport 4+ times per trip—check-in, security, lounge, gate—creating queues, friction, and redundant verification at every touchpoint.

### The Challenge:
Enable verify-once, travel-everywhere: a biometric or token-based identity (wallet) that follows the passenger, sharing verification status (not raw data) across airport and airline systems.

### Our solution

A system that replaces physical documents with secure facial recognition, creating a seamless journey from curb to gate. Your face becomes your
boarding pass, passport, and identity, verified once, recognized everywhere.

- Home Check-In
Register once via mobile app, scan passport and facial biometrics from home

- Biometric Enrollment
3-angle face scan securely linked to passport data in encrypted database

- Biometric Pass Created
Encrypted digital boarding pass generated and stored until you're in your plane

- Seamless Airport Flow
Face recognition at check-in, security, passport control, boarding, dutyfree

- Boarding Complete
Final verification at gate, no documents, no friction, just you
